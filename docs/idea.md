# TeledentX - Dental eClinic Platform

A platform that streamlines appointment scheduling, clinic management, and patient engagement for dental professionals and patients.

Built with React.js (Frontend), Node.js + Express (Backend), MongoDB Atlas (Database).

## Features include:

- **Appointment Scheduling:** Easy booking, modification, and cancellation of appointments.
- **Patient and Admin Dashboards:** Patients can view and manage their appointments, medical history, and billing. 
- Admins can manage patient records, appointments, and clinic operations.
- S**ecure Login & Authentication:** JWT-based authentication for secure access.
- **Treatment Details & Fees:** Patients can explore treatments and their associated costs.
- **Real-time Notifications:** Alerts for appointment confirmations, reminders, and updates.
- **Logs Management:** Admins can track appointment logs and patient interactions.
- **Billing Management:** Admins can generate invoices and track payments.
- **Order Medicines:** Admins can manage patient-specific medicine orders.
- **User-friendly Interface:** Designed for easy access across devices.

## Technology Stack: 
React.js, Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB Atlas.
